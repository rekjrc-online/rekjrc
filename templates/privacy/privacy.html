Contact: rekjrc@gmail.com

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Data You Provide

  • Username

2.2 Automatically Collected Data

3. How We Use Your Data

We process data to:

Legal bases (GDPR): contractual necessity, legitimate interest, consent (for analytics), legal obligation.

4. How We Share Your Data

We share data only with trusted providers for:

  • Hosting
  • Backups
  • Error logging

We never sell your data or use it for advertising.

If you are outside the United States, your data may be processed in the U.S. using industry-standard protection mechanisms.
